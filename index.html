Who We Are

We are a privately owned artisanal bakery and cafe, located in New York. We provide fresh, handmade, from scratch, pastries, bread, coffee & lunch containing no preservatives or additives. 
We source only the best local ingredients whenever possible, and believe in supporting other passionately run, independent businesses in our community.
